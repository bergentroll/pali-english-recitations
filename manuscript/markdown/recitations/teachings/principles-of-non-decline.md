## Principles of Non-Decline<a id="principles-of-non-decline"></a>
Aparihāniya-dhammā

> [Handa mayaṁ aparihāniya-dhamma-pāṭhaṁ bhaṇāmase]

Katame bhikkhave satta aparihāniyā dhammā

<div class="english">

> What bhikkus are the seven principles of non-decline?

</div>
