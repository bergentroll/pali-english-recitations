\begin{pali-hang}
[Evaṁ me sutaṁ] ekaṁ samayaṁ bhagavā gayāyaṁ viharati gayāsīse saddhiṁ bhikkhusahassena. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi:

\begin{pali-hang}
